Information Technology Stock App


This app is designed to show how opening price changes and the maximum closing price for selected stock(s) over a certain period of time. 


Features:
* Drop down to pick a stock 
* Select a date range
* Interactive graph output with option to add trend line
* Max closing price 


Instructions:
1. Select which stock/stocks you would like to learn more about
2. Input your desired date range
3. Check or uncheck the box indicating if you would like to add a linear trend line
4. View the graph and output for opening price  
5. Hover mouse over graph to look at specific days or prices 
6. View date and value of maximum closing price of the stock
